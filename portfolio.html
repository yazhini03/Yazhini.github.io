Life at Kumaraguru College of Technology

"Arise, awake and stop not till the goal is reached." - Swami Vivekananda

The first day at KCT was a mix of excitement and nervousness. Walking through the grand entrance, I was greeted by the lush green campus, modern buildings, and the buzzing energy of fellow students.

KCT's curriculum is a perfect balance of theory and practical knowledge. The state-of-the-art laboratories and well-stocked library became my second home as I delved into my engineering subjects.

The professors at KCT are not just teachers but mentors. Their approachability and willingness to help made learning a fulfilling experience. They encouraged us to think critically and innovate.

Being part of the Rotaract Club and various social initiatives, I realized the importance of giving back to society. These activities instilled a sense of responsibility and community spirit in me.

Like any journey, my time at KCT had its challenges. Balancing academics, extracurriculars, and personal life was tough at times. However, the supportive environment helped me overcome these hurdles. #kumaraguruCollegeofTechnology #informationtechnology hashtag #lifeatkct #gratitude
